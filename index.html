About Me

My name is Derek Stoeckmann, and I'm a guitar instructor, musician, and Arizona native. After earning my bachelor's degree in Psychology from Arizona State University, I ran a private music lesson business teaching guitar, bass, and ukulele to students of all ages. I also worked as a professional musician, leading a successful acoustic duo who played regularly at dozens of local wine bars, resorts, and restaurants—including SanTan Brewery right across the street!

Recently, I've gained an interest in web development and programming. Over the last two years, I've spent most of my spare time self-learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, as well as building websites for friends and family. Recently, I decided to enroll in the full-time coding bootcamp in order to take the next leap. I love learning, computers, and technology. I'm driven to start a career in web development!

I live in Tempe with my wonderful girlfriend and our two pets—a tri-paw'd rescue pup named George, and a Russian Blue cat named Diablo.
